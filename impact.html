Impact of SunShare Project

Environmental Impact

SunShare significantly reduces carbon emissions by shifting community energy consumption to renewable sources. By investing in shared solar projects, we've saved approximately 5,000 tons of CO2 emissions so far, equivalent to planting 50,000 trees.

Community Engagement

Our platform has empowered over 10,000 households to invest in solar energy and take control of their energy consumption. The collective effort of these communities is leading to a greener and more sustainable future.

Financial Growth

By participating in SunShare, communities have seen an average annual return on investment (ROI) of 12%. Many members have reported monthly energy cost savings of ₹2,000 to ₹5,000, creating long-term financial benefits.

Future Sustainability

The future of SunShare is focused on expanding its reach to rural areas and smaller towns, making solar energy accessible to all. Our next goal is to connect 100,000 households by 2026 and establish micro-grids for reliable energy distribution.

Social Benefits

In addition to environmental and financial gains, SunShare has fostered a sense of collaboration among community members. People from diverse backgrounds come together with a common goal of reducing their carbon footprint while benefiting financially.
