Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to the Community Solar Sharing Platform

FAQs

1. What is the Community Solar Sharing Platform?

The Community Solar Sharing Platform allows users to invest in shared solar projects and earn savings from the energy produced.

2. How do I invest in a solar project?

Users can sign up, check available projects, and buy shares to participate in a project.

3. How are my energy savings calculated?

Savings are calculated based on your share of the energy generated by the project, measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh).

4. Is my investment guaranteed?

While we guarantee savings based on energy production, the returns depend on overall project performance.

5. Can I track the energy produced by my investment?

Yes, you can log into your profile and track the energy production and savings in real-time.

6. How long does a typical solar project last?

Most solar projects have a lifespan of 20-25 years, during which time you'll continue to earn savings.

7. How do I withdraw my earnings?

Earnings can be withdrawn via bank transfer or reinvested into other projects on the platform.

8. Is there a minimum investment amount?

Yes, the minimum investment varies by project, but typically starts around $100.
